Virtual Moodle array

Services strategy

This field allows services strategy definition, when defining a virtual host.

It is possible to base the strategy on a default strategy, calked on the master virtual host's services and defined by an administrator. This option is automatically selected when the virtual host enables MNET without selecting a subnetwork, or when the virtual host disables MNET.

In a case of establishing a link with a subnetwork, services will be synchronized with one actual member of the subnetwork. It is necessary to remember that all members of a subnetwork are synchronized in terms of services.
